Chocolate chip cookies

Description

These cookies have the perfect fat/sugar/chocolate ratio!

Ingredients (24 cookies)

  • 125g of butter
  • 125g of sugar (half brown, half white)
  • 1 egg
  • 175g of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking soda (or 1 portion of baking powder)
  • 150g of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mix the sugar and the butter together with a fork until homogeneous. The butter should be soft but not liquid, to make mixing easier.
  2. Add the egg and mix again.
  3. Add the flour and the baking soda and mix until the dough is homegeneous.
  4. Finally, add the chocolate chips and mix.
  5. Pre-heat the oven at 180°C.
  6. Take two oven trays and put baking paper on them. With the dough, make little balls of the size of a walnut and place them on the tray. Space them enough so that they will have space to melt flat without fusionning. I usually make around 24 cookies.
  7. Put the trays in the oven at 180°C during about 10 minutes, until the edges start to be brown.
  8. Take them out of the oven. The cookies will solidify while cooling down.
  9. Enjoy your delicious cookies!
