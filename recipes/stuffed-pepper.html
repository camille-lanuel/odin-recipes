Stuffed pepper

Description

An easy recipe for the summer!

Ingredients (4 pers.)

  • 2 peppers
  • 1 goat chesse log (about 180g)
  • 250g of quark cheese
  • Fresh chives
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven at 180Â°C.
  2. Cut the peppers in half and core them.
  3. Mix the quark cheese, finely chopped chives, salt and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Add the goat's cheese cut into small cubes.
  5. Stuff the peppers with the mixture and bake for 30-45 min.
