Zucchini & gorgonzola pasta

Description

A simple, yet tasty recipe for gorgonzola lovers!

Ingredients

  • 150g of pasta
  • 1 zucchini
  • 150g of gorgonzola
  • 10cl of cream
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cut the zucchini in small cubes and cook them in a pan until they still are a bit crunchy.
  2. During that time, cook the pasta in salty water.
  3. Once the zucchini is cooked, set it aside.
  4. Melt the gorgonzola with the cream in the pan and add pepper.
  5. Once everything is cooked, mix all.
  6. Serve with Parmesan cheese.
